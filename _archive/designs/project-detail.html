Fintech · Platform redesign · 2024

Bnkly — A true partnership in innovation.

A collaborative engagement helping the Bnkly team refine their architecture, platform and mobile app for a new era of fintech — built to scale to a million customers without losing the human touch.

Client Bnkly Financial
Objective Re-platform & relaunch
Deliverables iOS, Android, web platform, design system
Duration 14 months

— 01 / The briefA platform that could grow up.

Bnkly came to us with a product that worked, a customer base that loved it, and an architecture quietly buckling under both. Three years of feature iteration had left a tangle of overlapping services, inconsistent design tokens, and a mobile experience that was lagging a generation behind the competition.

Our remit was clear: re-platform without disrupting customers, modernise the visual system without losing brand recognition, and ship a mobile app that would carry Bnkly into the next million accounts.

— 02 / What we deliveredA complete platform reset, end to end.

We started by stripping the design language back to first principles — a single typographic scale, a tighter colour system, and a component library generated from tokens. Engineering and design worked from the same source of truth from day one.

On the platform side, we collapsed seven services into three, introduced a new event-driven core, and rebuilt the customer dashboard around a clearer information hierarchy. The mobile app was rewritten on a shared design system, with biometric auth, instant transfers, and a redesigned onboarding that takes under ninety seconds.

Every release shipped behind a feature flag. Every change was measured. Every customer migration was opt-in until the very last week.

— 03 / Result+38% activation, half the support load.

Within four months of launch, activation was up 38%, daily active users up 22%, and support contact rate down by nearly half. Engineering velocity doubled — what used to take a sprint now takes a day. The platform now carries 2.4× the traffic on a third of the infrastructure cost.

More importantly, Bnkly has a system that compounds. Every team ships into the same design language. Every customer touchpoint feels like the same product. And the next million customers will land on a platform built for them, not adapted around them.
